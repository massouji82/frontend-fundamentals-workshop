Are Quokkas real?

Is Australia real? Are we even real? What is real? Are we all just a figment of someone's imagination? Or part of the matrix?

Yes, Quokkas are real. They seem too good to be true, and a bit too weird (like most animals in Australia, I'm still convinced that platypus' are a myth), but they do genuinely exist.

Can I have one?

Quokkas are only native to one island in Australia, and don't do well outside of their natural habitat. They're not great as pets, it would be similar to keeping a squirrel as a pet.
